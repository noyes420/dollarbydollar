Why Learn Financial Literacy?

Learning financial literacy is crucial for youth because it lays the foundation for a lifetime of financial well-being. By understanding the basics of managing money, young people can develop healthy financial habits early on, such as budgeting, saving, and making informed spending decisions. Financial literacy empowers youth to avoid common financial pitfalls and prepares them to navigate more complex financial situations as they grow older.
