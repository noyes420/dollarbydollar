Who We Are

At Dollar by Dollar, we are dedicated to empowering youth with financial literacy through engaging, accessible, and impactful resources. Our team is committed to helping the next generation achieve financial independence by equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in today's complex financial landscape.

Our Mission

Founded in 2024, Dollar by Dollar was born out of a desire to make financial literacy accessible to middle school students, empowering them with the knowledge and skills they need to navigate the world of money. We recognized that financial education often starts too late, leaving many young people unprepared for the financial decisions they'll face in adulthood.

Our mission is to change that by offering engaging, easy-to-understand resources and lessons that bring financial concepts to life for younger audiences. Through interactive workshops, online content, and community events, we aim to equip the next generation with the tools to build a secure financial future, one dollar at a time.

Our Vision

At Dollar by Dollar, we envision a future where every young person has the financial knowledge and skills they need to succeed.

Our goal is to make financial literacy fun, accessible, and empowering for students, helping them build a strong foundation for their future. We strive to be the go-to resource for parents and educators, supporting them in guiding the next generation toward financial independence and confidence.

Together, we can create a community where young people are prepared to make smart financial decisions that will benefit them for a lifetime.
